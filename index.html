Left Hand Typing Corrector

Type using only the left-hand side of the keyboard. The page treats each left-side key as either the actual key or its mirrored right-side key, then guesses the word you meant from context and a built-in dictionary.

What you type

Tip: when you need a right-hand letter, press the matching left-hand mirror key. For example, f can mean f or j, and e can mean e or i.
